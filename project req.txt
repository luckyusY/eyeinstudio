1. Executive Summary

This document defines the full technical and functional requirements for developing EyeInStudio.com, a complete enterprise-grade digital platform for Eye in Studio.

The platform will allow the business to:

* Establish strong online presence
* Showcase portfolio
* Accept bookings online
* Manage photo printing orders
* Sell framing and lamination services
* Store customer photos securely
* Manage business operations through admin dashboard

This is not just a portfolio website.
This is a complete digital business management ecosystem.
